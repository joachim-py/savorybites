Our Story

Founded in 2005, Savory Bites began as a small family-owned bistro with a passion for creating memorable dining experiences. What started as a humble kitchen with just five tables has now blossomed into one of the city's most celebrated culinary destinations.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest ingredients, honor traditional cooking techniques, and present every dish with artistic flair. Chef Marco Lombardi brings over 20 years of international experience to our kitchen, crafting menus that celebrate seasonal produce and global flavors.
